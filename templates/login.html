Why count calories?

By increasing your awareness of what you consume on a daily basis, calorie counting can aid in weight loss.
This might assist you in identifying eating habits that you might need to change in order to stay on track to accomplish your goals.
Or even if you don't want to lose weight but instead gain weight, it's still important to count calories in a healthy way and not overdo it.
